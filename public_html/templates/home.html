About Us

Welcome to Deep Dive Tutor! Deep Dive Tutor was built as a way for current students to receive tutoring from former students. All of the tutors have gone through the bootcamp and are very familiar with the course curriculum. Students can select from a variety of tutors based on the tutors strengths, and how it matches the individual students needs. If you are interested in signing up as a tutor, it is a great way to help promote student success as well as keep the curriculum fresh in the alumni's mind.
